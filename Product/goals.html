Giving Back

We value giving back to the community through volunteer groups, non-profit organizations, charity and other means.
Volunteering our time and effort does so much to help those in need and contributes to the common good.
KIVA participates in numerous campaigns and fundraisers.

Empowerment

We are lucky to live in a place where many ideas are visions are supported. However, this is not the case in many other areas of the world.
We hope that our loans and campaigns will encourage other individuals around the world to feel confident in their ideas and be hopeful.
We hope to empower women, children and minorities all across the world.

Opportunity

Ideas cannot be realized without opportunity. Our mission is to provide the opportuntity of business, equality, education, etc. all across the world.
More than often, money provides opportunity, as it can be used as an investment or deposit for something.
Our loans aid in providing individuals with the opportunity to start a business, receive healthcare, go to school, and much more.
